Call for Papers

Presentation proposals for satRday Cardiff are now open.

Submit your proposal

What are the benefits of giving a presentation?

  • Speakers will have their registration fees refunded.
  • Financial support may be possible for speakers.

Feel free to submit one or more proposal(s). We will be considering the following presentation formats:

  • Workshop [3 hours]
  • Standard Talk [30~45 min]
  • Lightning Talk [5~10 min]

When filling out the abstracts consider which of the following four sections would best suit your content.

  • Business - Use Case (e.g. “We at [good biz] did [great data project]”)
  • Business - Experience (e.g. “At [good biz] we believe in doing [technology/structure/process])
  • Code - Introduction (e.g. “How I learned to stop worrying and love git”, “A hitchhikers guide to the tidyverse”, “IDE another day”)
  • Code - Project (e.g. “I got some data on [something fun] and did a [cool thing]”)

If you are unsure about where your talk fits, please get in touch with us through email.
